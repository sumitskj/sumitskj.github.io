Swimming is placed on Wednesday and Sunday mornings — low CNS load, high recovery benefit, great for aerobic base.
Why these days?
WednesdayAfter Tue pull day — swim is active recovery, not fatigue
SundayRest day — low intensity swim loosens muscles, aids sleep
Swimming on a heavy gym day (Mon/Thu/Fri) would crush recovery. These two days are strategically low weight-training load.
